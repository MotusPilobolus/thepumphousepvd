About Us

Welcome to The Pump Room, the most fabulous gym in town! We believe in creating a safe and inclusive space for all queer and trans folks to work out and feel good.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals within the LGBTQ+ community to take control of their health and wellness through fitness and movement.

Our Vision

We envision a world where queer and trans folks feel confident and proud in their bodies, and where they can access the resources they need to maintain their physical and mental health.

Our Values

  • Empowerment
  • Inclusivity
  • Joy
  • Respect
  • Community
