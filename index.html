About Us

Your neighborhood gym just got a little gayer…


The Pump Room is Providence, RI’s first trans and queer-owned gym and juice bar concept that centers and celebrates every body that has historically been left out of the fitness narrative. Our space is a true judgement-free zone, where anyone of any identity, gender, size, and ability can move freely and confidently with other members of their community, and most importantly have FUN. With ethereal neon vibes evocative of 80’s malls, Miami Vice, and 80’s synth music, it’s always a party here. Come check us out when we’re up and running - your first visit is on us! ** We are currently in the fundraising stage and would appreciate your support!! Please visit our indiegogo page here to help fund this project and bring it to life! ** Pre-sale memberships are also available with early bird pricing!! Memberships will be limited to the first 150 people, so make sure you secure your spot ASAP!

Our Mission

To provide all intersections of the LGBTQ+ community and their allies with a safe space to strengthen and nourish their bodies and souls.

Our Vision

to make waves in the fitness industry by celebrating bodies that have historically been “othered” or deemed “undesirable” by traditional fitness standards.

Our Values

  • Embracing Diversity
  • Protecting our Community
  • Spreading Joy
  • Prioritizing Integrity
  • Respecting Individuality Empowerment

Owner Bio

Mar Keller (he/him) is a queer transgender man, NASM Certified Personal Trainer & Corrective Exercise Specialist, and a geek for all things 80s. He started Q GRIT Fitness, a personal training service for the LGBTQ+ community, in Brooklyn, NY in 2019 and is thrilled to be bringing the party up to Providence, RI! Mar has been featured as an instructor on fitness platforms such as Brrrn At Home and in various forms of media spotlighting transgender fitness instructors - most notably, Men’s Health Magazine (Pride issue 2023). As a former pastry chef, bartender, sommelier, and restaurant manager, The Pump Room Gym & Juice Bar is the lovechild of all of Mar’s interests and talents; He can’t wait to share this experience with you! Feel free to check out Q GRIT Fitness’s website here (hyperlink) to get to know him a bit better!

Juice Bar

The Juice Bar at The Pump Room is a one-of-a-kind juice bar/cafe by day and alcohol-free bar and social space by night. All juices are pressed fresh in house whenever possible using the highest quality ingredients, and each beverage is created with a culinary approach that elevates your flavor experience. Visit the juice bar to cool off after a workout, hit your macros with a smoothie, get work done, meet up with friends, or go on a sober date! Check out Our Menu
